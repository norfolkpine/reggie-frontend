# Opie AI Pricing Model Summary

## Overview

This document provides a comprehensive summary of the Opie AI pricing model, including our credit system, subscription tiers, and add-on services.

## Credit System

Our pricing model is built on a credit-based system that allows us to abstract the costs of the underlying services we use.

*   **1,000 credits = $1.00 USD**
