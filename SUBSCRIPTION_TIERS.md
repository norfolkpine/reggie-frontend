# Subscription Tiers for Opie AI

## Overview

Our pricing model is designed to be flexible and scalable, catering to a wide range of users from individuals to large enterprises. We offer four distinct tiers: **Free**, **Pro**, **Business**, and **Enterprise**.

## Tiers

### Free

*   **Price:** $0
*   **Target Audience:** Individuals and small teams who want to explore the platform.
*   **Credits:** 5,000 credits per month
*   **Features:**
    *   AI Chat (Simple Tasks)
    *   Vault File Storage (up to 2 GB)
    *   Standard Document Analysis
    *   Simple Workflows
    *   Collaborative Docs (up to 3 users)
    *   1 Team Workspace

### Pro

*   **Price:** $25 per user/month
*   **Target Audience:** Professionals and small teams who need more power and features.
*   **Credits:** 50,000 credits per month
*   **Features:**
    *   All features in **Free**
    *   AI Chat (Complex Tasks)
    *   Vault File Storage (up to 20 GB)
    *   Advanced Document Analysis
    *   Standard Workflows
    *   Collaborative Docs (up to 10 users)
    *   5 Team Workspaces
    *   Basic App Integrations

### Business

*   **Price:** $50 per user/month
*   **Target Audience:** Growing businesses and teams that require advanced features and support.
*   **Credits:** 150,000 credits per month
*   **Features:**
    *   All features in **Pro**
    *   Vault File Storage (up to 100 GB)
    *   Complex Workflows
    *   Collaborative Docs (unlimited users)
    *   Unlimited Team Workspaces
    *   Advanced App Integrations
    *   Priority Support

### Enterprise

*   **Price:** Custom
*   **Target Audience:** Large organizations with specific needs for security, support, and scalability.
*   **Credits:** Custom
*   **Features:**
    *   All features in **Business**
    *   Dedicated Infrastructure
    *   Custom Integrations
    *   Single Sign-On (SSO)
    *   Service Level Agreements (SLAs)
    *   Dedicated Account Manager
