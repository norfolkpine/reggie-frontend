# Add-On Services for Opie AI

## Overview

In addition to our subscription tiers, we offer a range of add-on services that allow users to customize their plans and access specialized features.

## Specialized Agents

*   **Price:** $20 per agent/month
*   **Description:** Access to pre-built, specialized agents for specific tasks, such as:
    *   **Legal Advisor:** An agent trained on legal documents and regulations.
    *   **Financial Analyst:** An agent that can analyze financial data and generate reports.
    *   **Marketing Guru:** An agent that can help with marketing copy and campaign ideas.

## Knowledge Bases

*   **Price:** $10 per knowledge base/month
*   **Description:** Create and manage custom knowledge bases that can be used to train your AI agents. This allows you to tailor the AI's responses to your specific needs and data.

## Complex Workflows

*   **Price:** $100 per workflow pack/month
*   **Description:** For users who need to run a high volume of complex, long-running workflows, we offer a workflow pack that includes:
    *   10,000 additional workflow actions
    *   Priority processing for workflow tasks

## Additional Credits

*   **Price:** $10 per 10,000 credits
*   **Description:** Users can purchase additional credits at any time to supplement their monthly allocation.
